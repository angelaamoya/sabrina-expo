about us

Discover the journey of Sabrina Annlynn Carpenter, a powerhouse artist who has captivated the global stage with her evolution from iconic television roles to pop music superstardom. With a streak of chart-topping hits including "Espresso," "Please Please Please," and the smash single "Manchild," Sabrina has cemented her status as a Grammy-winning force on the Billboard charts. Dive into the world of an artist who continues to redefine modern pop through her critically acclaimed albums, Short n' Sweet and Man's Best Friend, driven by her signature sound, unfiltered songwriting, and undeniable connection with fans worldwide.
